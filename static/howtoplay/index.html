How To Play

Overview of the game
Epidemic Go! is an interactive game where each player has their own virus and the goal is to infect as many players as possible. Once you are infected with another player's virus you will spread it as well if you are within its infection distance. Each player carries a different virus which has different traits for infection, these are important to keep in mind when trying to infect others!

Controls
Move your player by walking around the world! You can see your physical location on the map, as well as the other players

How to Score
If you are near another player (they are within your infection circle), you can press the infect button to infect them. You will receive 100 points for each infection and an addition 100 points each time that person spreads your virus.

Interact with Friends
Competition is central to this game, and it encourages you to spend time outside. The more you explore, the more people you can infect!
